### Workout Plan for a 43-Year-Old Male Beginner

**Overview:**
- **Age:** 43
- **Gender:** Male
- **Fitness Level:** Beginner
- **Goals:** Muscle Gain
- **Workout Duration:** 60 minutes per session
- **Workout Type:** Cardio
- **Equipment:** Full Gym
- **Workout Days:** 3
- **Intensity:** Low
- **Workout Time:** Morning
- **Warm-up/Cool-down:** Yes
- **Focus Areas:** Full Body

### Weekly Workout Plan

#### **Frequency:**
- 3 days a week (Monday, Wednesday, Friday)

#### **Duration:**
- 60 minutes per session

#### **Intensity:**
- Low

#### **Equipment:**
- Full Gym

#### **Focus Areas:**
- Full Body

### Daily Structure

#### **Monday: Full Body Workout A**
- **Duration:** 60 minutes
- **Warm-Up (10 minutes):** 
  - Arm Circles: 30 seconds
  - Leg Swings: 1 minute
  - March in Place: 3.5 minutes
  - Jumping Jacks: 3.5 minutes
  - Dynamic Stretching: 2 minutes

- **Main Workout (40 minutes):**
  - **Circuit 1 (Exercise Duration: 45 seconds, Rest: 30 seconds, Repeat: 2 times)**
    - Bench Press
    - Lat Pulldown
    - Dumbbell Shoulder Press
    - Bicep Curls
    - Tricep Extensions

- **Cool-Down (10 minutes):**
  - Shoulder Stretch: 30 seconds per arm
  - Standing Quad Stretch: 30 seconds per leg
  - Seated Forward Bend: 1 minute
  - Deep Breathing: 2 minutes

#### **Wednesday: Full Body Workout B**
- **Duration:** 60 minutes
- **Warm-Up (10 minutes):**
  - Side Lunges: 1 minute
  - Arm Circles: 30 seconds
  - High Knees: 3.5 minutes
  - Jumping Jacks: 3.5 minutes
  - Dynamic Stretching: 2 minutes

- **Main Workout (40 minutes):**
  - **Circuit 2 (Exercise Duration: 45 seconds, Rest: 30 seconds, Repeat: 2 times)**
    - Leg Press
    - Chest Press Machine
    - Dumbbell Deadlifts
    - Lateral Raises
    - Plank Hold

- **Cool-Down (10 minutes):**
  - Arm Across Chest Stretch: 30 seconds per arm
  - Standing Hamstring Stretch: 30 seconds per leg
  - Child’s Pose: 1 minute
  - Deep Breathing: 2 minutes

#### **Friday: Full Body Workout C**
- **Duration:** 60 minutes
- **Warm-Up (10 minutes):**
  - Dynamic Hip Flexor Stretch: 1 minute
  - Arm Circles: 30 seconds
  - Jumping Jacks: 3.5 minutes
  - Side Lunges: 1 minute
  - Dynamic Stretching: 2 minutes

- **Main Workout (40 minutes):**
  - **Circuit 3 (Exercise Duration: 45 seconds, Rest: 30 seconds, Repeat: 2 times)**
    - Dumbbell Step-Ups
    - Russian Twists
    - Dumbbell Sumo Squats
    - Chest Press Machine
    - Leg Curls/Leg Extensions

- **Cool-Down (10 minutes):**
  - Seated Forward Bend: 1 minute
  - Side Stretch: 30 seconds per side
  - Standing Calf Stretch: 1 minute
  - Deep Breathing: 2 minutes

### Weekly Summary
- **Monday:** Full Body Workout A
- **Wednesday:** Full Body Workout B
- **Friday:** Full Body Workout C
- **Saturday:** Rest Day or Optional Light Activity (e.g., walking or stretching)
- **Sunday:** Rest Day

This plan is designed to help you build muscle gradually while incorporating cardio elements for overall fitness. Remember to focus on proper form and gradually increase weights as you progress.
